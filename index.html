What I Do

  • As a SOC Level 1 Analyst, I serve as the first line of defense by monitoring and analyzing security tools such as SIEM, IDS/IPS, EDR, and firewall logs to identify suspicious activities and anomalies.
  • I perform initial triage of alerts to distinguish between false positives and true security incidents, while prioritizing them based on severity.
  • For basic issues, I handle documentation and resolution, and for advanced or unclear cases, I escalate to SOC Level 2 or Incident Response teams.
  • I also document all alerts, actions, and escalations through detailed incident tickets. In addition, I leverage threat intelligence to validate alerts and speed up detection of malicious activities, run initial checks on endpoints and user activity, and recommend immediate containment measures such as blocking IPs or disabling accounts when necessary.
